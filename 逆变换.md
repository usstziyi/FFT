### 在三维空间里的逆变换 —— 把零件进行组合

* 零件

$$ 
a_1 = x[0] \\
a_2 = x[1] \\
a_3 = x[2]
$$

* 组合工具
$$
e_1=\begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix},\quad
e_2=\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\0\end{bmatrix},\quad
e_3=\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 组合方式

$$
\mathbf{x}=\langle \mathbf{a},\mathbf{e}\rangle
=a_1\mathbf{e}_1+a_2\mathbf{e}_2+a_3\mathbf{e}_3
=x[0]\mathbf{e}_1+x[1]\mathbf{e}_2+x[2]\mathbf{e}_3
=x[0]\begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix} + 
x[1]\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\0\end{bmatrix} + 
x[2]\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}
=\sum_{i=0}^{3}a_i\mathbf{e}_i
=\begin{bmatrix}x[0] \\ x[1] \\ x[2]\end{bmatrix}
=x
$$

* 产物
$$
\mathbf{x}=
\begin{bmatrix}
x[0] \\ x[1] \\ x[2]
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### 在 DFT 里的逆变换

* 零件 （每种信号的峰值）
$$
X[0] = A_0N \\
X[1] = A_1N \\
X[2] = A_2N \\
X[3] = A_3N \\
\vdots\\
X[k] = A_kN
$$

* 组合工具（频率基）
$$
\mathbf{u}_0=
\begin{bmatrix}
1\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot0/N}\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot0\cdot 2/N}\\
\vdots\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot0(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix},\quad

\mathbf{u}_1=
\begin{bmatrix}
1\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot1/N}\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot1\cdot 2/N}\\
\vdots\\
e^{j2\pi \cdot1(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix},

\dots, \

\mathbf{u}_k=
\begin{bmatrix}
1\\
e^{j2\pi k/N}\\
e^{j2\pi k\cdot 2/N}\\
\vdots\\
e^{j2\pi k(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 组合方式

$$
X[0]\mathbf{u}_0 + X[1]\mathbf{u}_1 + \dots + X[N-1]\mathbf{u}_{N-1}
=A_0N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi \cdot0/N}\\e^{j2\pi \cdot0\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi \cdot0(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}
+A_1N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi \cdot1/N}\\e^{j2\pi \cdot1\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi \cdot1(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}
+\dots
+A_{N-1}N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi \cdot(N-1)/N}\\e^{j2\pi \cdot(N-1)\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi \cdot(N-1)(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}
=\sum_{k=2}^{N-1} X[k]\mathbf{u}_k
=N\begin{bmatrix}x[0] \\ x[1] \\ x[2] \\\vdots\\x[N-1]\end{bmatrix}
=N\mathbf{x}
$$

* 产物
$$
\mathbf{x}=
\begin{bmatrix}
x[0] \\ x[1] \\ x[2] \\\vdots\\x[N-1]
\end{bmatrix}
=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X[k]\mathbf{u}_k
$$

---
$$两个共轭复数相加，虚部抵消，结果是实数,以 x[0] 为例$$


$$
\begin{aligned}
x[0] &= A_0 + A_1 + \cdots + A_{N-1} \\
&=|A_0|e^{j\phi_0} + |A_1|e^{j\phi_1} + \cdots + |A_{N-1}|e^{j\phi_{N-1}} \\
&= \left(|A_0|e^{j\phi_0} + |A_{N-1}|e^{j\phi_{N-1}}\right) + \left(|A_1|e^{j\phi_1} + |A_{N-2}|e^{j\phi_{N-2}}\right) + \cdots \\
&= 2|A_0| + 2|A_1| + \cdots + 2|A_{(N-1)/2}| \\
&= 2\sum_{k=0}^{(N-1)/2}|A_k| \\
&= 实数
\end{aligned}
$$
