### 在三维空间里的逆变换 —— 把零件进行组合

* 零件

$$
a_1 = x[0],\quad a_2 = x[1],\quad a_3 = x[2]
$$

* 组合工具

$$
\mathbf{e}_1=\begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix},\quad
\mathbf{e}_2=\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\0\end{bmatrix},\quad
\mathbf{e}_3=\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 组合方式

$$
\mathbf{x}
= a_1\mathbf{e}_1 + a_2\mathbf{e}_2 + a_3\mathbf{e}_3
= x[0]\begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{bmatrix}
+ x[1]\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\0\end{bmatrix}
+ x[2]\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}
= \sum_{i=0}^{2} a_i\,\mathbf{e}_i
= \begin{bmatrix}x[0]\\x[1]\\x[2]\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 产物

$$
\mathbf{x}=\begin{bmatrix}x[0]\\x[1]\\x[2]\end{bmatrix}
$$

### 在 DFT 里的逆变换

* 零件（每种频率分量的幅值）

$$
X[0]=A_0\,N,\quad
X[1]=A_1\,N,\quad
X[2]=A_2\,N,\quad
\dots,\quad
X[k]=A_k\,N
$$

* 组合工具（频率基向量）

$$
\mathbf{u}_0=\begin{bmatrix}
1\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 0/N}\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 0\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 0(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix},\quad

\mathbf{u}_1=\begin{bmatrix}
1\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 1/N}\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 1\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 1(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix},\quad

\dots,\quad

\mathbf{u}_k=\begin{bmatrix}
1\\e^{j2\pi k/N}\\e^{j2\pi k\cdot 2/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi k(N-1)/N}
\end{bmatrix}
$$

* 组合方式

$$
\begin{aligned}
&X[0]\,\mathbf{u}_0 + X[1]\,\mathbf{u}_1 + \cdots + X[N-1]\,\mathbf{u}_{N-1}\\[6pt]
&= A_0 N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 0/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 0(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}
+ A_1 N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 1/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi\cdot 1(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}
+ \cdots
+ A_{N-1}N\begin{bmatrix}1\\e^{j2\pi(N-1)/N}\\\vdots\\e^{j2\pi(N-1)(N-1)/N}\end{bmatrix}\\[6pt]
&= \sum_{k=0}^{N-1} X[k]\,\mathbf{u}_k\\[6pt]
&= N\begin{bmatrix}x[0]\\x[1]\\x[2]\\\vdots\\x[N-1]\end{bmatrix}
= N\,\mathbf{x}
\end{aligned}
$$

* 产物

$$
\mathbf{x}
=\begin{bmatrix}x[0]\\x[1]\\x[2]\\\vdots\\x[N-1]\end{bmatrix}
=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}X[k]\,\mathbf{u}_k
$$

### 共轭对称性：虚部抵消，结果为实数

以 $x[0]$ 为例：

$$
\begin{aligned}
x[0]
&= A_0 + A_1 + \cdots + A_{N-1} \\[6pt]
&= |A_0|\,e^{j\phi_0} + |A_1|\,e^{j\phi_1} + \cdots + |A_{N-1}|\,e^{j\phi_{N-1}} \\[6pt]
&= \bigl(|A_0|\,e^{j\phi_0} + |A_{N-1}|\,e^{j\phi_{N-1}}\bigr)
 + \bigl(|A_1|\,e^{j\phi_1} + |A_{N-2}|\,e^{j\phi_{N-2}}\bigr)
 + \cdots \\[6pt]
&= 2|A_0| + 2|A_1| + \cdots + 2\bigl|A_{(N-1)/2}\bigr| \\[6pt]
&= 2\sum_{k=0}^{(N-1)/2}|A_k| \\[6pt]
&= \text{实数}
\end{aligned}
$$
